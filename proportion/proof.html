Proportion tutorial: the ol' single-exclamation proof of the Pythagorean Theorem.
"Behold!" which is short for, the outside length of the square is a, the side length of the red square is b, the area of the big square is a squared, the area of the red square is b squared, and the area of the blue triangle is .5*a*h, the height of the blue triangle. Show that a*a = b*b+4*(.5*a*h), and you're done.
