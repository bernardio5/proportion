Proportion: Lofting line segments.
To color in a section of a construction, you "loft". Here, two line segments are defined using four points on two lines. The "loft" command fills in the area between the two segments with the color specified. This can result in a convex 4-sided figure, but if the segments cross or pass close to each other, other shapes can be made.
