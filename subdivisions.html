Proportion tutorial: dividing up a line.
This construction is kind of silly, but interesting: how to construct any number of subdivisions of a line segment.

First, make two parallel diagonal lines through the endpoints of the segment. Then, draw identical circles along the lines; use their radii to space them equally. Finally, draw lines through the centers of the circles.

I say it's silly because you could just use "addParametric" and skip the construction. Pretty elegant, though.
