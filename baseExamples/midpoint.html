Proportion tutorial: construction of a midpoint.
This just demonstrates the use of the library to do some good old geometric construction. There are two points orbiting the center; their midpoint is constructed.

Draw circles of equal radius centered on the points; the line connecting the circles' points of intersectiion has all points equidistant from the two points. I think this works; check with Euclid if you like.
