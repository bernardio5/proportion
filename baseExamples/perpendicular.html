Proportion tutorial: Constructing a perpendicular line.
Building a perpendicular is much like finding a midpoint, except that the point that you build the line from is your starting point.

The smaller, unmoving circle is our start; we'll build a perpendicular from that point, on the line through that point and the center of the circle.

Use a second circle to get a third point on the line. Use that outer point to define the two large circles. Their intersection points give you the perpendicular you want.
